Wenfang Liao (Mars)

Welcome to my website, I am a Fashion Design and Development (BA) student in LCF. I have been studied in China, Australia and British. I have work experiences in the hospitality, technology, and fashion industry. Also has experience of creating and establishing own design brand “Freshman Design”. I am very passionate about fashion design that focuses not only on aesthetic pleasing but also on functional uses. In most of my collections, I aim to bring the Chinese culture to the designs to present my own culture to the world.
